Summer 2016 - Present


I am currently working under the supervision of Jorge Moreno, professor at Cal Poly Pomona. My current project consists of using high-resolution simulations of galaxy mergers to study their kinematics. More in detail, I am analzying the kinematic decoupling of the stellar and gas components highlighted in observations by CALIFA (Barrera-Ballesteros et al. 2015) and SAMI (Bloom et al. 2016) surveys.
